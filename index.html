Morty Smith

About Morty

Morty is a young, good-natured, and impressionable boy who can be somewhat easily manipulated. Despite his apparent lack of intellect, Morty has shown to be a good listener and follows directions well.

Morty's Characteristics

  1. Young
  2. Good Natured
  3. Easily Manipulated
  4. Impressionable Boy
  5. Low sense of self-esteem
